Identity

My drive as a designer is to see different perspectives, this is because different perspectives offer me new ways to think, which I can use to solve problems I was not able to before or in different ways, be it faster, prettier or easier. They also allow me to understand others easier, letting me design or explain specifically to them.
My favourite thing about design is the iterative design process, as I like to learn from my mistakes. This method works well for me as it teaches me fast, in a way I can understand, the only negative is that I want more iterations than I generally have time for.
My passion is to 3D model, I like it and I do it often, I use Blender to model and I hope to use program, or method of creation in my future.

Vision

I see design as a means that can help others or improve processes, something that keeps repeating and something that is never finished since the world and the people for which you design also always change. I feel that designers must never stop learning, that there is never an endpoint, and that one can always improve a product or themselves. I want to work more with 3D-modelling be it with printers or the actual models since I think these will become more necessary in the future. For now, I am working towards making models for film or for games, this is because I like it when I see a design made in a way that I would have thought about, this also ties to my identity, where I said that my drive was to see different perspectives.
